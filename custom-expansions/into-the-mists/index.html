Into the Mists

The mists part and Pandaria is revealed. A full custom expansion set in the ancient continent of Pandaria, featuring all twelve classes, a new Monk hero, and mechanics inspired by World of Warcraft: Mists of Pandaria.

Encounter the Sha, brave the Tillers' fields, face the Thunder King, and discover what lies hidden within the mists. New keywords, new factions, and new legendaries await.

Featuring: The Monk

Into the Mists introduces the Monk as a brand-new playable class — the first new class added to our custom Hearthstone roster. Masters of Chi energy, Monks blend healing, martial strikes, and board empowerment into a unique playstyle unlike any existing class.

Chi

Chi is a new resource exclusive to the Monk. You accumulate Chi by playing certain cards or triggering effects. Cards with Chi(X) unlock a stronger version of their effect when you spend the required Chi — giving Monk an engine of escalating power throughout the game.
