Into the Mists

The mists part and Pandaria is revealed. A full custom expansion set in the ancient continent of Pandaria, featuring all twelve classes, a new Monk hero, and mechanics inspired by World of Warcraft: Mists of Pandaria.

Encounter the Sha, brave the Tillers' fields, face the Thunder King, and discover what lies hidden within the mists. New keywords, new factions, and new legendaries await.
