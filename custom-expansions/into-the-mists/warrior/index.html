Warrior

Factions: Hozen • Garrosh • Niuzao
Themes: Arms • Fury • Armor generation • Costs Armor instead of Mana

Warrior in Into the Mists turns Armor into a second resource. Some cards cost Armor instead of Mana, rewarding you for staying tanky. Halve incoming damage, double your offensive output, and end the game with the fury of Grommash Hellscream.
