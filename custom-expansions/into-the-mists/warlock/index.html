Warlock

Factions: Sha • Klaxxi • Mogu
Themes: Fire Spells • Burning Embers • Disabling opponent deck • Mana disruption

Warlock in Into the Mists wields the volatile power of Burning Embers. These cursed tokens accumulate in your hand, dealing more damage each turn — but also hurting you if left unplayed. Master the timing, and convert that self-damage into unstoppable summons.
